# The Way of MOS

**A path for living in coherence.**

The Way of MOS is an open framework for measuring, navigating, and witnessing the coherence of a human life through three irreducible aspects: **Mystery** (awareness and presence), **Odyssey** (shadow work and creation), and **Sanctity** (love and connection).

> *The Practice in One Breath: Be present. Feel. Love.*

The framework is in **[WAY-OF-MOS.md](./WAY-OF-MOS.md)** — one page, the whole thing distilled to its essence. For the canonical articulation in beautiful presentation, visit **[wayofmos.com](https://wayofmos.com)**.

*(A Complete edition — the fuller geometry: the measure, the coherence formula, the twenty-seven states of the Cube, and the recognition across wisdom traditions — is in preparation and will be added here.)*

- A coaching modality built on the framework
- A book, course, or talk that teaches it

## Lineage

The Way of MOS rests in a much longer lineage of contemplative traditions. It attempts to honor what is universal across many wisdom paths — Mystery, Odyssey, and Sanctity have been independently named, in different vocabularies, by every major contemplative tradition. The Way of MOS is one articulation among many. It claims to be useful, not unique.
